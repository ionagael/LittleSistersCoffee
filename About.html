About

The story of Little Sister's Coffee begins with a movie. In 2012, my sister and I filmed a scene in a small movie. In that scene, my character begs his sister for money to open up a small coffee shop. Ten years later, that coffee shop became a reality, in the real world! Little Sister's Coffee memorializes that moment.
