About

In 2012, my sister and I filmed a scene in a small movie. The director of the movie knew I liked coffee so much that he allowed me to film a scene where my character asks his sister for money to open a small coffee shop. Ten years later, that coffee shop became a reality, in the real world! Little Sister's Coffee is a tribute to that scene.
